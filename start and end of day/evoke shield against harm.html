Bismillāh al-Ladhī Lā Yaḍurru

In the Name of Allah with Whose Name Nothing Harms

📖 Morning and Evening Protection

The Prophet ﷺ taught that reciting this supplication three times every morning and evening serves as an absolute shield. Whoever says it sincerely will be granted complete immunity from sudden afflictions and all earthly or celestial harm throughout their day or night.

The Narration Chain

ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān reported:
"The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: 'There is no servant who says in the morning of every day and the evening of every night...'"
The Arabic Narration
مَا مِنْ there is no mā min
عَبْدٍ servant ʿabdin
يَقُولُ who says yaqūlu
ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān al-Umawī

Full name: ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān ibn Abī al-ʿĀṣ (d. 35 AH).

The third Rightly Guided Caliph (Amīr al-Mu'minīn) and son-in-law of the Prophet ﷺ, known as Dhū al-Nūrayn. He was celebrated for his deep modesty, immense generosity, and compilation of the standard Mushaf of the Qur'ān.

Role here: He heard this direct instruction from the lips of the Messenger of Allah ﷺ and transmitted it to his children and companions.

Part 1 · Covenant of Protection
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ in the name of Allah Bismi-llāh
الَّذِي with Whose al-ladhī
لَا يَضُرُّ nothing harms lā yaḍurru
مَعَ اسْمِهِ with His Name maʿas-mihī
شَيْءٌ anything shay'un
فِي الْأَرْضِ in the earth fil-arḍi
وَلَا and not wa lā
فِي السَّمَاءِ in the heaven fis-samā'i
In the Name of Allah, with Whose Name there is protection against every kind of harm in the earth or in the heaven,
Ṣaḥīḥ Riyad as-Salihin, Book 15, Hadith 50 (No. 1457).
Part 2 · Divine Attributes
وَهُوَ and He wa Huwa
السَّمِيعُ the All-Hearing as-Samīʿu
الْعَلِيمُ the All-Knowing al-ʿAlīm
and He is the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing.
Ṣaḥīḥ Riyad as-Salihin, Book 15, Hadith 50 (No. 1457).
✅ Source & Authenticity

Narrated by ʿUthmān ibn ʿAffān (RA). Recorded by Abū Dāwūd (No. 5088) and At-Tirmidhī (No. 3388). Formally compiled in Riyad as-Salihin, Book 15, Hadith 50 (No. 1457). Grade: Ḥasan Ṣaḥīḥ according to Imam At-Tirmidhī.
