Ruqyah for Physical Illness

Authentic Remedies for Pain from the Sunnah

📖 Islamic Medicine Context

Seeking spiritual remedies alongside physical medical treatment represents the complete approach of the Sunnah. These two prayers demonstrate how the Prophet ﷺ and his companions actively sought relief from severe physical afflictions by placing their hands on the location of pain and appealing directly to Allah's absolute power and mercy.

Part 1 · The Remedy for Localised Body Pain
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ in the name of Allah Bismi-llāh
In the name of Allah — Recite ×3
أَعُوذُ i seek refuge Aʿūdhu
بِعِزَّةِ اللَّهِ in Allah's might bi-ʿizzatillāh
وَقُدْرَتِهِ and His power wa-qudratihī
مِنْ شَرِّ from the evil min sharri
مَا of what mā
أَجِدُ i find and feel ajidu
وَأُحَاذِرُ and i fear wa-uḥādhir
I seek refuge in the might and power of Allah from the evil of what I feel and fear — Recite ×7
Ṣaḥīḥ Sahih Muslim, Book 39, Hadith 2202.
Part 2 · The Comprehensive Prayer of Shifa
اللَّهُمَّ o Allah Allāhumma
رَبَّ النَّاسِ Lord of mankind Rabba-nṇās
أَذْهِبِ الْبَأْسَ remove the harm adhhibi-l-ba's
اشْفِ heal and cure Ishfi
أَنْتَ You Anta
الشَّافِي the Healer ash-Shāfī
لَا شِفَاءَ there is no cure lā shifā'a
إِلَّا شِفَاؤُكَ except Your cure illā shifā'uka
شِفَاءً a healing shifā'an
لَا يُغَادِرُ that leaves not lā yughādiru
سَقَمًا any sickness saqamā
O Allah, Lord of mankind, remove the harm. Heal, for You are the Healer. There is no healing except Your healing— a healing that leaves no disease behind.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sahih al-Bukhari, Book 76, Hadith 5743.
✅ Source & Authenticity

Dua 1 (Localised Pain): Narrated by Uthman ibn Abi al-As al-Thaqafi. Sahih Muslim, Book 39, Hadith 2202. Grade: Ṣaḥīḥ. The story behind this du'a notes that Uthman came to the Prophet ﷺ complaining of an agonizing pain in his body that had afflicted him ever since he embraced Islam. The Prophet ﷺ instructed him to place his hand directly over the painful site, recite Bismillah three times, and repeat the formula of seeking refuge seven times; Uthman reported that he followed this prescription exactly and Allah completely cured his chronic pain.

Dua 2 (Comprehensive Shifa): Narrated by Aisha (RA). Sahih al-Bukhari, Book 76, Hadith 5743 & Sahih Muslim, Book 39, Hadith 2191. Grade: Ṣaḥīḥ. The transmission history details that whenever any member of the household fell sick, the Prophet ﷺ would step forward, place his blessed right hand over the ailing area, and read this comprehensive plea for healing. It was his established practice when performing physical house visits to the sick, showing his method of physically touching the area of disease to offer comforting support and divine words of protection.
