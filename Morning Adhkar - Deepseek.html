أذكار الصباح — Morning Adhkār

Authentic Supplications from the Sunnah

The morning adhkār (remembrances and supplications) are among the greatest means of protection, forgiveness, and drawing closer to Allah. The Prophet ﷺ encouraged reciting them between Fajr and sunrise. Whoever says them with faith and sincerity will be guarded from harm until evening, by Allah’s leave.

The Prophet ﷺ said: “Whoever says in the morning: ‘Bismillah illadhi la yadurru ma’a ismihi shay’un fi’l-ard wa la fi’l-sama’ wa huwa as-samee‘ ul-‘aleem’ three times, nothing will harm him until evening.” (Abū Dāwūd 5088, Ṣaḥīḥ)
