00:00 · Opening Supplication
رَبَّنَا وَتَعَالَيْتَ Our Lord, Exalted are You
يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالْإِكْرَامِ O Possessor of Majesty and Honor

Our Lord, Exalted are You, O Possessor of Majesty and Honor.
اللَّهُمَّ يَا مُقَلِّبَ الْقُلُوبِ O Allah, Turner of hearts
ثَبِّتْ قُلُوبَنَا عَلَى دِينِكَ keep our hearts firm upon Your religion
Allāhumma yā muqallibal-qulūbi thabbit qulūbanā ʿalā dīnik
O Allah, O Turner of the hearts, keep our hearts firm upon Your religion.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَسْأَلُكَ رِضَاكَ وَالْجَنَّةَ O Allah, we ask You for Your pleasure and Paradise
وَنَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ سَخَطِكَ وَالنَّارِ and we seek refuge in You from Your wrath and the Fire
Allāhumma innā nas'aluka riḍāka wal-jannah, wa naʿūdhu bika min sakhaṭika wan-nār
O Allah, we ask You for Your pleasure and Paradise, and we seek refuge in You from Your wrath and the Fire.
سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ Glory be to Allah, All praise to Allah
وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ and Allah is the Greatest

01:05 · Exaltation & Surah Al-Fatihah
اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ كَبِيرًا Allah is Greatest, truly
وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ كَثِيرًا and abundant praise to Allah
وَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ بُكْرَةً وَأَصِيلًا and glory to Allah morning and evening
Allāhu akbaru kabīrā, wal-ḥamdu lillāhi kathīrā, wa subḥānallāhi bukratan wa aṣīlā
Allah is the Greatest, truly; and praise be to Allah, abundantly; and glory be to Allah, morning and evening.
لَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ There is no power nor strength except with Allah, the High, the Great
lā ḥawla wa lā quwwata illā billāhil ʿaliyyil ʿaẓīm
There is no power and no strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Most Great.

الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ Sovereign of the Day of Recompense
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ It is You we worship and You we ask for help
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ Guide us to the straight path
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor
غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ not of those who have evoked [Your] anger or of those who are astray
Surah Al-Fatihah (1:1-7)
Recitation of Surah Al-Fatihah.

وَعَلَى آلِ سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ and upon the family of our master Muhammad
O Allah, send blessings upon our master Muhammad and upon the family of our master Muhammad.

Greetings of peace sent upon the Prophet ﷺ, the righteous Companions, and the Family of the Prophet. (Followed by a repetition of Surah Al-Fatihah).
04:58 · Master of Forgiveness & Refuge
اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ خَلَقْتَنِي O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no deity except You, You created me
أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا صَنَعْتُ I seek refuge in You from the evil of what I have done
فَاغْفِرْ لِي فَإِنَّهُ لَا يَغْفِرُ الذُّنُوبَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ so forgive me, for none forgives sins except You
Excerpts from Sayyid al-Istighfar (The Master of Seeking Forgiveness).
مَا شَاءَ اللَّهُ كَانَ، وَمَا لَمْ يَشَأْ لَمْ يَكُنْ What Allah wills comes to pass, and what He does not will does not occur
أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ نَفْسِي I seek refuge in You from the evil of my soul
وَمِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ دَابَّةٍ أَنْتَ آخِذٌ بِنَاصِيَتِهَا and from the evil of every creature whose forelock You hold
Affirmation of Divine Decree and seeking refuge from all harm.

06:58 · Reliance (Tawakkul)
وَأُفَوِّضُ أَمْرِي إِلَى اللَّهِ ۚ إِنَّ اللَّهَ بَصِيرٌ بِالْعِبَادِ And I entrust my affair to Allah. Indeed, Allah is Seeing of [His] servants
Qur'ān 40:44. Entrusting all affairs strictly to Allah.
لَقَدْ جَاءَكُمْ رَسُولٌ مِنْ أَنْفُسِكُمْ عَزِيزٌ عَلَيْهِ مَا عَنِتُّمْ There has certainly come to you a Messenger from among yourselves. Grievous to him is what you suffer...
فَإِنْ تَوَلَّوْا فَقُلْ حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۖ But if they turn away, say, "Sufficient for me is Allah; there is no deity except Him...
عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ ۖ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ ...upon Him I have relied, and He is the Lord of the Great Throne."
Qur'ān 9:128-129. [Repeated] Recited for reliance on Allah.
08:07 · Surah Al-Hashr & Asma Ul-Husna
أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ السَّمِيعِ الْعَلِيمِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ I seek refuge in Allah, the Hearing, the Knowing, from the accursed devil
Repeated seeking of refuge before recitation.
لَوْ أَنْزَلْنَا هَٰذَا الْقُرْآنَ عَلَىٰ جَبَلٍ لَرَأَيْتَهُ خَاشِعًا مُتَصَدِّعًا مِنْ خَشْيَةِ اللَّهِ If We had sent down this Qur'an upon a mountain, you would have seen it humbled and coming apart from fear of Allah.
Recitation of the final verses of Surah Al-Hashr (59:21-24), containing the beautiful names of Allah.
09:50 · Surah Al-Baqarah Excerpts
الم ۝ ذَٰلِكَ الْكِتَابُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ ۛ هُدًى لِلْمُتَّقِينَ Alif, Lam, Meem. This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah
Opening verses of Surah Al-Baqarah (2:1-5).
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْحَيُّ الْقَيُّومُ Allah - there is no deity except Him, the Ever-Living, the Sustainer of [all] existence.
وَهُوَ الْعَلِيُّ الْعَظِيمُ And He is the Most High, the Most Great.
Ayatul Kursi (2:255). The greatest verse in the Qur'an for protection.
آمَنَ الرَّسُولُ بِمَا أُنْزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مِنْ رَبِّهِ وَالْمُؤْمِنُونَ The Messenger has believed in what was revealed to him from his Lord, and [so have] the believers.
رَبَّنَا وَلَا تُحَمِّلْنَا مَا لَا طَاقَةَ لَنَا بِهِ ۖ وَاعْفُ عَنَّا وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا وَارْحَمْنَا Our Lord, and burden us not with that which we have no ability to bear. And pardon us; and forgive us; and have mercy upon us.
The closing verses of Surah Al-Baqarah (2:285-286).

لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ الْمَلِكُ الْحَقُّ الْمُبِينُ There is no god but Allah, the Sovereign, the Clear Truth

19:17 · Extensive Salawat
اللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِ مُحَمَّدٍ فِي الْأَوَّلِينَ وَالْآخِرِينَ O Allah, send blessings upon Muhammad and the family of Muhammad among the first and the last generations.
Continuous sending of peace and blessings upon the Prophet ﷺ in all realms and times until the Day of Judgment.
إِنَّ اللَّهَ وَمَلَائِكَتَهُ يُصَلُّونَ عَلَى النَّبِيِّ ۚ يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا صَلُّوا عَلَيْهِ وَسَلِّمُوا تَسْلِيمًا Indeed, Allah confers blessing upon the Prophet, and His angels [ask Him to do so]. O you who have believed, ask [Allah to confer] blessing upon him and ask [Allah to grant him] peace.
Qur'ān 33:56.

Recitation of Ayat an-Nur (The Verse of Light - 24:35).

Qur'ān 17:82. Verses recited specifically for physical and spiritual healing.
28:52 · Closing Supplications
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّا نَسْأَلُكَ الْعَفْوَ وَالْعَافِيَةَ فِي الدِّينِ وَالدُّنْيَا وَالْآخِرَةِ O Allah, we ask You for forgiveness and well-being in our religion, worldly affairs, and the Hereafter.
Comprehensive closing prayers asking for pardon, safety, good ending (Husn al-Khatimah), and entrance to Paradise.
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ Our Lord, grant us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and protect us from the punishment of the Fire.
The universal concluding Qur'ānic Du'ā (2:201) followed by a final recitation of Surah Al-Fatihah.
