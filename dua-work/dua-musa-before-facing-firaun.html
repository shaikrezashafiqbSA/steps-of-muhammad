Du'ā — Rabbish-raḥlī ṣadrī

Supplication for Clarity and Speech

This is the beautiful prayer of Prophet Musa (AS) when he was commanded by Allah to face Pharaoh. It is a profound plea for spiritual expansion, ease in difficult tasks, and the ability to communicate effectively so that others may understand one's message.

Sūrat Ṭāhā · 25–28
رَبِّ My Lord
اشْرَحْ expand
لِي for me
صَدْرِي my chest
Rabbish-raḥlī ṣadrī
My Lord, expand for me my breast [with assurance]
Qur'ān Sūrat Ṭāhā (20:25).
Quran.com 20:25 ↗
وَيَسِّرْ and ease
لِي for me
أَمْرِي my affair
wa yassir lī amrī
And ease for me my task
Qur'ān Sūrat Ṭāhā (20:26).

وَاحْلُلْ and untie
عُقْدَةً a knot
مِّن from
لِّسَانِي my tongue
waḥ-lul ʿuqdatam-mil-lisānī
And untie the knot from my tongue
Qur'ān Sūrat Ṭāhā (20:27).
Quran.com 20:27 ↗
يَفْقَهُوا they may understand
قَوْلِي my speech
yaf-qahū qawlī
That they may understand my speech
