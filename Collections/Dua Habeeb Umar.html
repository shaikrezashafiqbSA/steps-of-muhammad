دُعَاءُ الْحَمْد

The Supplication of Praise

📖 About This Du'ā

This comprehensive supplication opens with praise of Allah, followed by abundant blessings upon the Prophet ﷺ, then a series of personal petitions for protection, well-being, and steadfastness. It is recited in gatherings of dhikr and after obligatory prayers in the tradition of the Ba'Alawi scholars of Hadramawt, particularly as transmitted through Ḥabīb ʿAlī al-Ḥabashī and connected chains.

Part 1 · Opening Basmala & Ḥamd
بِسْمِ اللهِ in the name of Allah Bismi-llāh
الرَّحْمَنِ the Most Gracious ar-Raḥmān
الرَّحِيمِ the Most Merciful ar-Raḥīm
In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.
Qur'ān Sūrat al-Fātiḥah (1), Āyah 1.
الْحَمْدُ لِلهِ the praise is for Allah Al-ḥamdu lillāh
رَبَّ الْعَالَمِينَ Lord of the worlds Rabbi-l-ʿālamīn
All praise is for Allah, the Lord of all the worlds.
Qur'ān Sūrat al-Fātiḥah (1), Āyah 2.
Part 2 · Praise that Befits His Bounty
حَمْدًا a praise Ḥamdan
يُوَافِي that fulfills yūwāfī
نِعَمَهُ His favours niʿamahu
وَيُكَافِئُ and rewards for wa yukāfiʾu
مَزِيدَهُ His increase mazīdah
Praise that fulfills His favours and is commensurate with His increase in bounties.
Part 3 · Praise Befitting His Majesty
يَا رَبَّنَا O our Lord Yā Rabbanā
لَكَ الْحَمْدُ for You is the praise laka-l-ḥamd
كَمَا يَنْبَغِي as is befitting kamā yanbaghī
لِجَلَالِ وَجْهِكَ for Your Face's majesty li-jalāli wajhik
وَعَظِيمِ سُلْطَانِكَ and Your great dominion wa ʿaẓīmi sulṭānik
O our Lord, for You is all praise, as is befitting the majesty of Your Face and the greatness of Your dominion.
Part 4 · Glory Beyond All Enumeration
سُبْحَانَكَ glory be to You Subḥānaka
لَا نُحْصِي we cannot enumerate lā nuḥṣī
ثَنَاءً عَلَيْكَ praise upon You thanāʾan ʿalayk
أَنْتَ You are Anta
كَمَا أَثْنَيْتَ as You have praised kamā athnayta
عَلَى نَفْسِكَ upon Yourself ʿalā nafsik
Glory be to You — we cannot enumerate all praise upon You; You are as You have praised Yourself.
Ṣaḥīḥ Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim, Book 4, Hadith 1092. Narrated by ʿĀʾishah (RA).
Part 5 · Praise Until His Pleasure
فَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ so for You is praise Fa-laka-l-ḥamd
حَتَّى تَرْضَى until You are pleased ḥattā tarḍā
وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ and for You is praise wa laka-l-ḥamd
إِذَا رَضِيتَ when You are pleased idhā raḍīta
وَلَكَ الْحَمْدُ and for You is praise wa laka-l-ḥamd
بَعْدَ الرَّضَى after the pleasure baʿda-r-raḍā
So for You is all praise until You are pleased, and for You is praise when You are pleased, and for You is praise even after Your pleasure.
Part 6 · Ṣalawāt upon the Prophet ﷺ
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
صَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ send blessings and peace ṣalli wa sallim
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا upon our master ʿalā Sayyidinā
مُحَمَّدٍ Muḥammad Muḥammad
فِي الْأَوَّلِينَ among the first ones fi-l-awwalīn
O Allah, send blessings and peace upon our master Muḥammad among the first ones.
وَصَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ and send blessings and peace wa ṣalli wa sallim
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا upon our master ʿalā Sayyidinā
مُحَمَّدٍ Muḥammad Muḥammad
فِي الْآخِرِينَ among the last ones fi-l-ākhirīn
And send blessings and peace upon our master Muḥammad among the last ones.
وَصَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ and send blessings and peace wa ṣalli wa sallim
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا upon our master ʿalā Sayyidinā
مُحَمَّدٍ Muḥammad Muḥammad
فِي كُلِّ وَقْتٍ in every time fī kulli waqt
وَحِينٍ and moment wa ḥīn
And send blessings and peace upon our master Muḥammad in every time and every moment.
وَصَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ and send blessings and peace wa ṣalli wa sallim
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا upon our master ʿalā Sayyidinā
مُحَمَّدٍ Muḥammad Muḥammad
فِي الْمَلَإِ الْأَعْلَى in the highest assembly fi-l-malaʾi-l-aʿlā
إِلَى يَوْمِ الدِّينِ until the Day of Judgement ilā yawmi-d-dīn
And send blessings and peace upon our master Muḥammad in the highest assembly, until the Day of Judgement.
وَصَلِّ وَسَلِّمْ and send blessings and peace wa ṣalli wa sallim
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا upon our master ʿalā Sayyidinā
مُحَمَّدٍ Muḥammad Muḥammad
حَتَّى تَرِثَ الْأَرْضَ until You inherit the earth ḥattā taritha-l-arḍ
وَمَنْ عَلَيْهَا and whoever is upon it wa man ʿalayhā
وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ and You are the best wa anta khayru
الْوَارِثِينَ of the inheritors al-wārithīn
And send blessings and peace upon our master Muḥammad, until You inherit the earth and all upon it, for You are the best of inheritors.
Part 7 · Entrusting All Affairs to Allah
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
إِنَّا نَسْتَحْفِظُكَ we ask You to guard innā nastaḥfiẓuk
وَنَسْتَوْدِعُكَ and entrust to You wa nastawdiʿuk
أَدْيَانَنَا our deen adyānanā
وَأَنْفُسَنَا and our souls wa anfusanā
وَأَمْوَالَنَا and our wealth wa amwālanā
وَأَهْلَنَا and our families wa ahlanā
وَكُلَّ شَيْءٍ and every thing wa kulla shayʾ
أَعْطَيْتَنَا You have given us aʿṭaytanā
O Allah, we ask You to safeguard us and we entrust to You our deen, our souls, our wealth, our families, and everything You have given us.
Part 8 · Seeking Protection
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
اجْعَلْنَا وَإِيَّاهُمْ make us and them ijʿalnā wa iyyāhum
فِي كَنَفِكَ in Your shelter fī kanafik
وَأَمَانِكَ and Your security wa amānik
وَعِيَاذِكَ and Your refuge wa ʿiyādhik
مِنْ كُلِّ شَيْطَانٍ from every devil min kulli shayṭān
مَرِيدٍ rebellious marīd
وَجَبَّارٍ عَنِيدٍ and every obstinate tyrant wa jabbārin ʿanīd
O Allah, place us and them in Your shelter, Your security, and Your refuge, from every rebellious devil and every obstinate tyrant.
وَذِي عَيْنٍ and the evil eye wa dhī ʿayn
وَذِي بَغْيٍ and wrongdoing wa dhī baghī
وَذِي حَسَدٍ and envy wa dhī ḥasad
وَمِنْ شَرِّ and from the evil wa min sharri
كُلِّ ذِي شَرٍّ of every evil-doer kulli dhī sharr
إِنَّكَ verily You Innaka
عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ over every thing ʿalā kulli shayʾ
قَدِيرٌ Powerful Qadīr
And from the one with the evil eye, the wrongdoer, and the envious one, and from the evil of every possessor of evil — for verily You are over all things Powerful.
Part 9 · Well-Being & Steadfastness
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
جَمِّلْنَا beautify us jammilnā
بِالْعَافِيَةِ with well-being bi-l-ʿāfiyah
وَالسَّلَامَةِ and safety wa-s-salāmah
وَحَقِّقْنَا and make us realise wa ḥaqqiqnā
بِالتَّقْوَى through piety bi-t-taqwā
وَالِاسْتِقَامَةِ and steadfastness wa-l-istiqāmah
O Allah, adorn us with well-being and safety, and grant us true realisation through piety and steadfastness.
وَأَعِذْنَا and protect us wa aʿidhnā
مِنْ مُوجِبَاتِ from the causes of min mūjibāti
النَّدَامَةِ regret an-nadāmah
فِي الْحَالِ in the present fi-l-ḥāl
وَالْمَآلِ and the final outcome wa-l-maʾāl
إِنَّكَ verily You are Innaka
سَمِيعُ الدُّعَاءِ the Hearer of supplication Samīʿu-d-duʿāʾ
And protect us from all that causes regret, in the present and in the final outcome — for verily You are the Hearer of supplication.
Part 10 · Closing Ṣalawāt & Seal
وَصَلِّ اللَّهُمَّ and send blessings O Allah wa ṣalli Allāhumma
بِجَلَالِكَ by Your majesty bi-jalālik
وَجَمَالِكَ and Your beauty wa jamālik
عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ upon our master Muḥammad ʿalā Sayyidinā Muḥammad
وَعَلَى آلِهِ and upon his family wa ʿalā ālih
وَصَحْبِهِ أَجْمَعِينَ and all his companions wa ṣaḥbihī ajmaʿīn
And send blessings, O Allah, by Your majesty and beauty, upon our master Muḥammad and upon his family and all his companions.
وَارْزُقْنَا and grant us wa-rzuqnā
كَمَالَ الْمُتَابَعَةِ perfection of following kamāla-l-mutābaʿah
لَهُ him lahu
ظَاهِرًا وَبَاطِنًا outwardly and inwardly ẓāhiran wa bāṭinan
يَا أَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ O Most Merciful yā Arḥama-r-Rāḥimīn
And grant us the perfection of following him, outwardly and inwardly, O Most Merciful of those who show mercy.
Part 11 · Qur'ānic Seal
بِفَضْلِ by the grace of Bi-faḍli
سُبْحَانَ رَبِّكَ glory be to your Lord Subḥāna Rabbik
رَبِّ الْعِزَّةِ Lord of Honour Rabbi-l-ʿizzah
عَمَّا يَصِفُونَ above what they describe ʿammā yaṣifūn
By the grace of: Glory be to your Lord, the Lord of Honour, far above what they describe.
Qur'ān Sūrat aṣ-Ṣāffāt (37), Āyah 180.
وَسَلَامٌ and peace wa salāmun
عَلَى الْمُرْسَلِينَ upon the Messengers ʿala-l-mursalīn
وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ and praise be to Allah wa-l-ḥamdu lillāh
رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ Lord of the worlds Rabbi-l-ʿālamīn
And peace be upon the Messengers, and all praise is for Allah, the Lord of all the worlds.
Qur'ān Sūrat aṣ-Ṣāffāt (37), Āyāt 181–182.
✅ Source & Authenticity

This supplication is a compiled du'ā recited in the gatherings (majālis) of the Ba'Alawi tradition of Ḥaḍramawt, particularly as transmitted by the scholars of the Ḥabāʾib. Its individual phrases draw from authentic hadith — including phrases found in Ṣaḥīḥ Muslim (e.g. the "lā nuḥṣī thanāʾan ʿalayk" formula, narrated by ʿĀʾishah RA) — and from the Qur'ānic verses of Sūrat al-Fātiḥah and Sūrat aṣ-Ṣāffāt (37:180–182). The ṣalawāt phrases are in the well-established tradition of mursala ṣalawāt recited in scholarly gatherings. The du'ā as a whole is a practised, transmitted supplication of the scholars (mashāyikh), not a single hadith.
