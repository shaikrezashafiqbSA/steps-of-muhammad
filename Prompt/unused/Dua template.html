Du'ā for Success

Supplication of the Prophet ﷺ

Divine Names
يَا O
حَيُّ the Ever-Living
يَا O
قَيُّومُ the Self-Subsisting
Yā Ḥayyu Yā Qayyūm
O The Ever-Living, O The Self-Subsisting
The Request
بِرَحْمَتِكَ through Your Mercy
أَسْتَغِيثُ I seek help
bi-raḥmatika astaghīth
I seek help through Your Mercy
أَصْلِحْ Set right
لِي for me
شَأْنِي my affairs
كُلَّهُ all of them
aṣliḥ lī sha'nī kullahu
Set right all my affairs
The Preservation
وَلَا and do not
تَكِلْنِي leave me
إِلَىٰ to
نَفْسِي myself
طَرْفَةَ for the blinking
عَيْنٍ of an eye
wa lā takilnī ilā nafsī ṭarfata ‘ayn
And do not leave me to myself even for the blinking of an eye

✨ Virtue: The Prophet (ﷺ) once asked Fatimah (RA), "What could prevent you from listening to the advice I give you?" He then instructed her to recite this du'a every morning and evening to ensure all affairs are rectified by Allah's mercy.

• Imam Al-Mundhiri classified this as Sahih (Authentic) in At-Targhib wa’t-Tarhib.
• Sheikh al-Albani graded it as Hasan (Sound) in Silsilah as-Sahihah (No. 227).

Anas bin Malik reported:
"Whenever a matter would distress him, the Prophet (ﷺ) would say: ‘Yā Ḥayyu yā Qayyūm, bi-raḥmatika astaghīth’ (O Living, O Self-Sustaining Sustainer! In Your Mercy do I seek relief)."

He also said: “The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: ‘Be constant with: Yā Dhal-Jalāli wal-Ikrām (O Possessor of Majesty and Honor).’”
