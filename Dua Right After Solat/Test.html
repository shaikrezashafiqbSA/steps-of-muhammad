ذِكْرٌ بَعْدَ السَّلَامِ

Remembrance After the Salām

📖 When This Is Recited

This dhikr was recited by the Prophet ﷺ immediately after completing the salām (taslīm) at the end of every prayer. It is a comprehensive declaration of tawḥīd, gratitude, and sincere devotion — affirming Allah's oneness, His dominion, His praise, and the worshipper's exclusive dedication to Him.

Part 1 · Declaration of Oneness
لَا إِلَٰهَ there is no god Lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
وَحْدَهُ alone waḥdah
لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ no partner for Him lā sharīka lah
There is none worthy of worship except Allah alone, with no partner or associate.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
Part 2 · Dominion and Praise
لَهُ الْمُلْكُ His is the dominion lahul-mulk
وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ and His is the praise wa lahul-ḥamd
وَهُوَ and He is wa huwa
عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ over every thing ʿalā kulli shay'in
قَدِيرٌ all-powerful qadīr
His is the dominion, and to Him belongs all praise, and He is able to do all things.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
Part 3 · No Power Except With Allah
لَا حَوْلَ there is no power lā ḥawla
وَلَا قُوَّةَ and no strength wa lā quwwata
إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ except with Allah illā billāh
الْعَظِيمِ the Almighty al-ʿAẓīm
There is no power and no strength except with Allah the Almighty.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
Part 4 · Exclusive Worship
لَا إِلَٰهَ there is no god lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
لَا نَعْبُدُ we worship not lā naʿbudu
إِلَّا إِيَّاهُ except Him alone illā iyyāh
There is none worthy of worship except Allah, and we worship none but Him.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
Part 5 · The Source of All Blessing
أَهْلَ النِّعْمَةِ source of blessing ahlan-niʿmah
وَالْفَضْلِ and of grace wal-faḍl
وَالثَّنَاءِ الْحَسَنِ and good praise wath-thanā'il-ḥasan
The source of blessing and kindness, and the One deserving of all good praise.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
Part 6 · Sincere Devotion
لَا إِلَٰهَ there is no god lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
مُخْلِصِينَ sincere in faith mukhlisīn
لَهُ الدِّينَ to Him in religion lahud-dīn
وَلَوْ كَرِهَ even though detest wa law kariha
الْكَافِرُونَ the disbelievers al-kāfirūn
There is none worthy of worship except Allah, and we are sincere in faith and devotion to Him, even though the disbelievers detest it.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13, Hadith 161 (No. 1339). Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340.
✅ Source & Authenticity

Narrated by ʿAbdullāh ibn al-Zubayr (RA), who heard it from the Messenger of Allah ﷺ and transmitted it from the minbar. Recorded in Sunan an-Nasā'ī, Book 13 (Kitāb al-Sahw), Hadith 161 (No. 1339); Vol. 2, Book 13, Hadith 1340. Grade: Ṣaḥīḥ (Dār al-Salām).
