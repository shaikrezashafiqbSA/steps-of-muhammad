Wirid Selepas Solat

Prophetic Remembrances After Prayer

This collection follows the traditional Sunnah sequence of dhikr recited immediately after the formal prayer. It begins with seeking forgiveness, transitions into glorification, and concludes with powerful prayers of protection (Dua Abu Darda and the Healing Bismillah).
