Du'ā — Yā Ghaniyyu Aghnīnā

Supplication for Sufficiency, Repentance & the Best of Both Worlds

This beautiful du'ā weaves together praises of Allāh using His beautiful names (al‑Asmā’ al‑Ḥusnā), the powerful plea of Prophet Yūnus (21:87), and a request for lawful provision, obedience, and the ultimate good in this life and the Hereafter (2:201). It is sealed with ṣalawāt upon the Prophet Muḥammad ﷺ.

The Prophet ﷺ said: “Shall I not teach you a supplication through which, if you are afflicted with any distress, Allah will grant you relief?” Then he mentioned the du‘ā of Dhū an‑Nūn (Yūnus): Lā ilāha illā anta subḥānaka innī kuntu minaẓ‑ẓālimīn. (Tirmidhī)
