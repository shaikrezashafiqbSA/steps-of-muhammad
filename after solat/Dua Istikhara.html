Full Du'ā al-Istikhāra

Seeking the Best from Allah's Decree

This prayer is performed for any matter where a choice is to be made. After praying two rak'ahs, recite this supplication with presence of heart, acknowledging that only Allah knows the unseen future.

1 · Seeking Ability & Knowledge
اللَّهُمَّO Allah
إِنِّيverily I
أَسْتَخِيرُكَseek Your guidance
بِعِلْمِكَby Your knowledge
وَأَسْتَقْدِرُكَseek Your ability
بِقُدْرَتِكَby Your power
وَأَسْأَلُكَand I ask You
مِنْfrom
فَضْلِكَYour bounty
الْعَظِيمِthe Great
Allāhumma innī astakhīruka bi-ʿilmika wa astaqdiruka bi-qudratika wa as’aluka min faḍlika-l-ʿaẓīm
O Allah, I seek Your guidance through Your knowledge, and I seek ability through Your power, and I ask You for Your great bounty.
فَإِنَّكَfor You
تَقْدِرُhave power
وَلَاand not
أَقْدِرُI have power
وَتَعْلَمُand You know
وَلَاand not
أَعْلَمُI know
وَأَنْتَand You are
عَلَّامُKnower of
الْغُيُوبِthe Unseen
Fa-innaka taqdiru wa lā aqdiru, wa taʿlamu wa lā aʿlamu, wa anta ʿallāmu-l-ghuyūb
For You have power and I have none; You know and I know not; and You are the Knower of the unseen.
2 · Affirming the Good
اللَّهُمَّO Allah
إِنْif
كُنْتَYou
تَعْلَمُknow
أَنَّthat
هَذَاthis
الأَمْرَmatter
خَيْرٌis good
لِيfor me
فِيin
دِينِيmy religion
وَمَعَاشِيmy life
وَعَاقِبَةِand outcome
أَمْرِيof affairs
عَاجِلِهِthe immediate
وَآجِلِهِand the later
Allāhumma in kunta taʿlamu anna hādhā-l-amra khayrun lī fī dīnī wa maʿāshī wa ʿāqibati amrī, ʿājilihi wa ājilih
O Allah, if You know this matter to be good for me in my religion, my livelihood, and the outcome of my affairs—both immediate and later...
فَاقْدُرْهُordain it
لِيfor me
وَيَسِّرْهُease it
لِيfor me
ثُمَّthen
بَارِكْbless
لِيfor me
فِيهِin it
faqdurhu lī wa yassirhu lī thumma bārik lī fīh
...then ordain it for me, make it easy for me, and then bless it for me.

And if You know this matter to be evil for me in my religion, my livelihood, and the outcome of my affairs—both immediate and later...
فَاصْرِفْهُturn it
عَنِّيfrom me

عَنْهُfrom it

لِيَfor me
الْخَيْرَthe good
حَيْثُwherever
كَانَit may be
ثُمَّthen
أَرْضِنِيmake content
بِهِwith it
fa-ṣrifhu ʿannī waṣrifnī ʿanhu waqdur liya-l-khayra ḥaythu kāna thumma arḍinī bih
...then turn it away from me and turn me away from it; and ordain for me the good wherever it may be and make me content with it.

Ṣaḥīḥ Reported in Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī (1162). This is the gold standard for seeking guidance in the Islamic tradition.
