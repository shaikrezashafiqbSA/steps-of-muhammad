Dhikr Baʿda al-Ṣalāh

Remembrance After the Prayer

📖 When to Recite

Recited immediately after completing each obligatory prayer — beginning with three times saying Astaghfirullāh, then this supplication once.

Narrator
Thawbān reported:
"When the Messenger of Allah ﷺ finished his prayer, he would seek forgiveness three times and say…"
The Arabic Chain
كَانَ رَسُولُ اللَّهِ the Messenger of Allah ﷺ used to kāna Rasūlu-llāh
إِذَا انْصَرَفَ when he finished idhā-nṣarafa
مِنْ صَلَاتِهِ from his prayer min ṣalātih
اسْتَغْفَرَ ثَلَاثًا seek forgiveness three times istaghfara thalāthan
وَقَالَ and say wa qāla
On Seeking Forgiveness
Walīd said: "I asked al-Awzāʿī: How is the seeking of forgiveness done?" He replied: "You say: Astaghfirullāh, Astaghfirullāh."
Part 1 · Seeking Forgiveness
أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ I seek forgiveness from Allah Astaghfiru-llāh
I seek forgiveness from Allah — ×3
Part 2 · The Supplication
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
أَنْتَ السَّلَامُ You are Peace Anta-s-salām
وَمِنْكَ السَّلَامُ and from You is peace wa minka-s-salām
تَبَارَكْتَ Blessed are You tabārakta
يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ O Possessor of Majesty yā dha-l-jalāl
وَالْإِكْرَامِ and Honour wa-l-ikrām
O Allah, You are Peace and from You comes peace. Blessed are You, O Possessor of Majesty and Honour.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sahih Muslim, Book 5, Hadith 171 (No. 591).
✅ Source & Authenticity

Narrated by Thawbān (RA), a freed slave and devoted Companion of the Prophet ﷺ. Recorded in Sahih Muslim, Book 5 (The Book of Mosques), Hadith 171 (No. 591). Grade: Ṣaḥīḥ.

Thawbān ibn Bajdad (RA)

Status: Ṣaḥābī (Companion of the Prophet ﷺ). Originally a prisoner of war, he was freed by the Prophet ﷺ and thereafter never left his service.

He was known for his intense love of the Prophet ﷺ — narrators recorded that he would grow visibly distressed at the thought of separation from him in the Hereafter, which prompted the revelation of hadith about intercession.

Narrations: Over 100 hadith attributed to him. His reports appear in all major collections.
