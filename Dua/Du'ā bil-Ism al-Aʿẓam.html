Du'ā bil-Ism al-Aʿẓam

Supplication by the Greatest Name of Allah

📖 The Greatest Name of Allah

The Prophet ﷺ heard a man supplicating using the attributes of Allah found in Sūrat al-Ikhlāṣ and declared that he had called upon Allah by His Greatest Name — al-Ism al-Aʿẓam. Scholars consider this one of the most powerful forms of supplication a believer can make. It may be recited at any time, especially when making an earnest and urgent du'ā to Allah.

Part 1 · The Address
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
إِنِّي indeed I innī
أَسْأَلُكَ ask You as'aluka
بِأَنَّكَ by Your being bi-annaka
أَنْتَ اللَّهُ You are Allah Anta-llāh
O Allah, indeed I ask You by virtue of Your being Allah —
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan Ibn Mājah, Book 34, Hadith 31 (No. 3857).
Part 2 · The Divine Attributes
الْأَحَدُ the One al-Aḥad
الصَّمَدُ the Self-Sufficient aṣ-Ṣamad
الَّذِي لَمْ يَلِدْ who begets not alladhī lam yalid
وَلَمْ يُولَدْ nor was begotten wa lam yūlad
وَلَمْ يَكُنْ and there is not wa lam yakun
لَهُ to Him lahu
كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ any equal kufuwan aḥad
the One, the Self-Sufficient Master, who begets not nor was begotten, and there is none co-equal or comparable to Him.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan Ibn Mājah, Book 34, Hadith 31 (No. 3857).
Part 3 · The Prophetic Testimony
لَقَدْ سَأَلَ اللَّهَ he has asked Allah laqad sa'ala-llāh
بِاسْمِهِ الْأَعْظَمِ by His Greatest Name bi-smihi al-Aʿẓam
الَّذِي إِذَا سُئِلَ بِهِ which if asked by it alladhī idhā su'ila bih
أَعْطَى He gives aʿṭā
وَإِذَا دُعِيَ بِهِ and if called upon by it wa idhā duʿiya bih
أَجَابَ He answers ajāb
He has asked Allah by His Greatest Name — which if He is asked thereby He gives, and if He is called upon thereby He answers.
Ṣaḥīḥ Sunan Ibn Mājah, Book 34, Hadith 31 (No. 3857).
✅ Source & Authenticity

Narrated by Buraidah ibn al-Ḥuṣayb (RA), transmitted through his son ʿAbdullāh ibn Buraidah. Recorded in Sunan Ibn Mājah, Book 34, Hadith 31 (No. 3857). Grade: Ṣaḥīḥ (Darussalam). The hadith is corroborated by parallel narrations in Sunan Abī Dāwūd (No. 1493) and Sunan at-Tirmidhī (No. 3475), all attesting to the same ruling of the Prophet ﷺ regarding this supplication.
