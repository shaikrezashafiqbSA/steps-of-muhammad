أَعْمَالُ أَيَّامِ ذِي الْحِجَّةِ

Amalan 1–10 Zulhijjah

📖 Keutamaan Hari-Hari Zulhijjah

Sepuluh hari pertama bulan Zulhijjah adalah hari-hari yang paling mulia dalam setahun. Rasulullah ﷺ bersabda bahawa tiada hari-hari yang amalan soleh di dalamnya lebih dicintai Allah daripada sepuluh hari ini. Bacaan-bacaan berikut diamalkan pada setiap hari dalam tempoh tersebut sebagai bentuk taqarrub (mendekatkan diri) kepada Allah.

Amalan 1 · Tahlīl dengan Sifat-Sifat Kekuasaan
لَا إِلَٰهَ there is no god Lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
وَحْدَهُ alone waḥdah
لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ no partner for Him lā sharīka lah
لَهُ الْمُلْكُ His is the dominion lahul-mulk
وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ and His the praise wa lahul-ḥamd
يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ He gives life and death yuḥyī wa yumīt
وَهُوَ حَيٌّ and He is Ever-Living wa huwa ḥayyun
لَا يَمُوتُ He does not die lā yamūt
بِيَدِهِ الْخَيْرُ in His hand is all good biyadihil-khayr
وَهُوَ عَلَى and He is over wa huwa ʿalā
كُلِّ شَيْءٍ every thing kulli shay'in
قَدِيرٌ all-powerful qadīr
There is none worthy of worship except Allah alone, with no partner. His is the dominion and all praise. He gives life and causes death, and He is Ever-Living, never dying. In His hand is all good, and He is over all things powerful.
Amalan 2 · Syahadah dengan Sifat Keesaan dan As-Samad
أَشْهَدُ I bear witness Ashhadu
أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ that there is no god an lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
وَحْدَهُ alone waḥdah
لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ no partner for Him lā sharīka lah
إِلَٰهًا وَاحِدًا one God ilāhan wāḥidan
أَحَدًا uniquely one aḥadan
صَمَدًا the Eternal Refuge ṣamadan
لَمْ يَتَّخِذْ He has not taken lam yattakhidh
صَاحِبَةً a companion/wife ṣāḥibatan
وَلَا وَلَدًا nor a child wa lā waladan
I bear witness that there is none worthy of worship except Allah alone, with no partner — one God, uniquely One, the Eternal Refuge, who has not taken a companion nor a child.
Amalan 3 · Syahadah dengan Sūrat al-Ikhlāṣ
أَشْهَدُ I bear witness Ashhadu
أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ that there is no god an lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illā-llāh
وَحْدَهُ alone waḥdah
لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ no partner for Him lā sharīka lah
أَحَدٌ صَمَدٌ One, the Eternal Aḥadun Ṣamad
لَمْ يَلِدْ He did not beget lam yalid
وَلَمْ يُولَدْ nor was He begotten wa lam yūlad
وَلَمْ يَكُنْ لَهُ and there is not for Him wa lam yakun lahū
كُفُوًا أَحَدٌ any equal, none kufuwan aḥad
I bear witness that there is none worthy of worship except Allah alone, with no partner — One, the Eternal Refuge, who did not beget, nor was He begotten, and there is none equal to Him.

✅ Sumber & Kesahihan

Amalan 1–4 merupakan bacaan tahlīl dan syahadah yang bersumberkan hadith-hadith sahih serta ayat-ayat al-Qur'ān (termasuk Sūrat al-Ikhlāṣ, 112:1–4). Diamalkan secara khusus pada sepuluh hari pertama Zulhijjah sebagai bentuk zikir dan taqarrub kepada Allah. Sila sahkan teks Arab dan rumi dengan ustaz atau rujukan mu'tabar sebelum disebarkan.

Amalan 1–10 Zulhijjah – Chunk 2

Amalan 1–10 Zulhijjah · Part 2

📖 Supplications 5–7

This section contains three short but profound supplications (Amalan 5–6) expressing complete reliance upon Allah, followed by Amalan 7 — a seven-part du'ā for the relief of debt, each phrase invoking one of Allah's attributes of mercy, generosity, and nearness.

Amalan 5 · Sufficiency of Allah and His Answer
حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ sufficient for me is Allah Ḥasbiya-llāhu
وَكَفَى and He suffices wa kafā
سَمِعَ اللَّهُ Allah has heard samiʿa-llāhu
لِمَنْ دَعَا whoever called upon Him liman daʿā
لَيْسَ وَرَاءَهُ beyond Him there is laysa warā'ahū
مُنْتَهَى no end muntahā
Allah is sufficient for me and He suffices. Allah has heard whoever called upon Him. Beyond Him there is no end.
Amalan 6 · Reliance and Safety Through Allah
مَنْ تَوَكَّلَ whoever relies man tawakkala
عَلَى اللَّهِ upon Allah ʿala-llāhi
كُفِيَ is sufficed kufiya
وَمَنِ اعْتَصَمَ and whoever holds fast wa maniʿtaṣama
بِاللَّهِ to Allah billāhi
نَجَا is saved najā
Whoever relies upon Allah is sufficed by Him. And whoever holds fast to Allah is saved.
Amalan 7 · Du'ā for the Relief of Debt (A–G)
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
فَرَجُكَ Your relief farajuka
الْقَرِيبُ the near one al-qarīb
O Allah, Your relief is near.
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
سِتْرُكَ Your veil sitruka
الْحَصِينُ the impenetrable al-ḥaṣīn
O Allah, Your protective veil is impenetrable.
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
مَعْرُوفُكَ Your kindness maʿrūfuka
الْقَدِيمُ the ancient al-qadīm
O Allah, Your kindness is ancient and enduring.
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
عَوَائِدُكَ Your blessings ʿawā'iduka
الْحَسَنَةُ the beautiful al-ḥasanah
O Allah, Your recurring blessings are beautiful.
اللَّهُمَّ O Allah Allāhumma
عَطَاؤُكَ Your gift ʿaṭā'uka
الْحَسَنُ الْجَمِيلُ beautiful and splendid al-ḥasanul-jamīl
O Allah, Your gift is beautiful and splendid.
يَا قَدِيمَ O Ancient One of yā qadīma
الْإِحْسَانِ beneficence al-iḥsān
إِحْسَانُكَ Your beneficence iḥsānuka
الْقَدِيمُ is ancient al-qadīm
O Ancient One of beneficence — Your beneficence is eternal and without beginning.
يَا دَائِمَ O Eternal One of yā dā'ima
الْمَعْرُوفِ kindness al-maʿrūf
مَعْرُوفُكَ Your kindness maʿrūfuka
دَائِمٌ is ever-lasting dā'im
O Eternal One of kindness — Your kindness is everlasting.
✅ Source & Authenticity

Amalan 5 and 6 are short expressions of tawakkul (reliance on Allah) drawn from the tradition of Islamic supplication. Amalan 7 (A–G) is a du'ā for the relief of debt, invoking Allah's attributes of nearness, mercy, and generosity.
