Istighfār Rajab

Habib Abdullah Al-Haddad · Rajab Litany of Forgiveness

This comprehensive litany of forgiveness was composed by Habib Abdullah ibn ʿAlawi Al-Haddad (1634–1720 CE), the great Ba'Alawi scholar and saint of Ḥaḍramawt, Yemen. It is recited specifically in the sacred month of Rajab — the month of Allah — as a structured turning back to Him before Sha'bān and Ramaḍān. Each section addresses a different dimension of sin and repentance, moving from general forgiveness through specific categories: hidden sins, broken oaths, neglected obligations, and the relapse after repentance. It closes with prayers upon the Prophet ﷺ across all realms of existence.
