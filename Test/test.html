Amalan 10 Zulhijjah

DEEDS OF THE FIRST 10 DAYS

📖 Merit of Dhu'l-Hijjah

These supplications and remembrances are traditionally recited during the first ten days of Dhu'l-Hijjah, which are the most beloved days to Allah for performing righteous deeds.

Part 1 · Declaration of Tawhid
لَا إِلَٰهَ there is no god Lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illa-llāh
وَحْدَهُ alone waḥdahū
لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ no partner for Him lā sharīka lahū
لَهُ الْمُلْكُ to Him is dominion lahu-l-mulk
وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ to Him is praise wa lahu-l-ḥamd
يُحْيِي وَيُمِيتُ He gives life and death yuḥyī wa yumīt
وَهُوَ حَيٌّ and He is Ever-Living wa huwa ḥayyun
لَا يَمُوتُ He does not die lā yamūt
بِيَدِهِ الْخَيْرُ in His hand is goodness biyadihi-l-khayr
وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ and He is over all things wa huwa ʿalā kulli shayʾin
قَدِيرٌ all-powerful qadīr
There is no god but Allah, alone without partner. To Him belongs dominion and praise, He gives life and causes death, and He is Ever-Living and never dies. In His Hand is all goodness, and He has power over all things.

Part 2 · The Witness of Faith
أَشْهَدُ أَنْ لَا إِلَٰهَ i bear witness no god ashhadu an lā ilāha
إِلَّا اللَّهُ except Allah illa-llāh
إِلَٰهًا وَاحِدًا One God ilāhan wāḥidan
أَحَدًا صَمَدًا Unique, Eternal aḥadan ṣamadan
لَمْ يَتَّخِذْ He has not taken lam yattakhidh
صَاحِبَةً a companion ṣāḥibatan
وَلَا وَلَدًا nor a son wa lā waladan
I bear witness that there is no god but Allah, the One and Only, the Unique and Eternal, who has taken neither companion nor son.

Part 3 · Enumerated Praises
لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ no god but Allah Lā ilāha illa-llāh
عَدَدَ اللَّيَالِي number of nights ʿadada-l-layālī
وَالدُّهُورِ and the ages wa-d-duhūr
There is no god but Allah, as many times as the number of nights and ages.
لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ no god but Allah Lā ilāha illa-llāh
عَدَدَ أَمْوَاجِ number of waves ʿadada amwāji
الْبُحُورِ the seas al-buḥūr
There is no god but Allah, as many times as the number of waves in the oceans.
